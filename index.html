About me

I have a degree in Marketing from the School of Business Sciences of the Polytechnic Institute of Setúbal and took a course in Web Design (Responsive Web) at ETIC. I have a curious nature and I love discovering and learning new things. I found in Web Design a place where I can express my creativity and, at the same time, try to give a great experience to everyone who sees my work. I am at the beginning of my career as a Web Designer and I have a long learning path ahead of me, but it is by walking that the path is made. See my work and give me your opinion if you wish. All constructive feedback is welcome!
